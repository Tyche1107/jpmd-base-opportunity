# What I Would Do If I Were JPMorgan Kinexys Leadership

**Context:** JPMD deployed on Base (public blockchain) in Nov 2025. 3 months later: 0% market share, $0 public TVL. Estimated $10M deployment cost. Complete failure.

---

## Immediate Actions (0-30 Days)

### 1. Shut Down Base Deployment
**Hard Truth:** This experiment failed.

**Action:**
- Formally sunset JPMD on Base
- Redirect all resources to Canton Network (already succeeding at $2B/day)
- Public statement: "We're consolidating JPMD on Canton Network to better serve institutional clients"

**Why:**
- Continuing wastes resources (engineering, marketing, legal)
- 0% market share after 3 months = structural failure, not execution failure
- Canton proves the product works—just not on public chains

**Cost Savings:**
- $2M-$5M annual maintenance (devs, audits, legal, marketing)
- Reallocate to Canton expansion

### 3. Redirect Base Strategy to Permissionless Products
**If JPM wants Base exposure, don't use JPMD.**

**Alternative Approaches:**

**Option A: Partner with Circle**
- Integrate USDC into JPM's custody/settlement systems
- Offer "JPM-custodied USDC on Base" to clients
- Let Circle handle permissionless layer, JPM handles institutional custody

**Option B: Launch JPM Index Products**
- Tokenized index funds on Base (permissionless)
- E.g., "JPM Emerging Markets Token Index"
- Separate from JPMD (different regulatory category)

**Option C: Pure Infrastructure Play**
- Don't issue tokens
- Build Base-compatible custody/settlement tools
- Sell to other issuers

**Recommendation:** Option A (fastest, lowest risk)

## What NOT to Do

### ❌ Don't Make JPMD Permissionless
**Temptation:** "Just open it up to everyone!"

**Why Not:**
- Regulatory nightmare (deposit token without banking license)
- Destroys JPMD's value prop (institutional-grade, JPM-backed)
- Cannibalize JPM's other products
- Legal risk (securities laws, money transmission)

**If You Want Permissionless:**
- Build a DIFFERENT product
- Partner with someone else (e.g., Circle)
- Don't repurpose JPMD

### ❌ Don't Keep Base Deployment Alive "Just in Case"
**Sunk Cost Fallacy**

0% market share after 3 months = structural failure.  
6 more months won't change physics.

Cut losses. Reallocate.

### ❌ Don't Blame Execution
**Tempting narrative:** "We just need better marketing for JPMD on Base!"

**Reality:** No amount of marketing fixes structural mismatch.

Permissioned token + Public blockchain = Incompatible.

Admit the strategy was wrong, not the team.
